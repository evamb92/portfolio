Welcome to the personal website of Eva Mbengue, a multilingual journalist covering the news in New York City and in Paris.

Eva is pursuing a Master degree in Journalism at CELSA Sorbonne-Université. She is currently taking class at the Craig Newmark Graduate School of Journalism in New York.

Eva contributed to La Provence, a local newspaper based in Marseille. La Voix du Nord and the Belgian newspaper Le Soir covering business and culture.

She is a French citizen who partially grew up in London, United Kingdom and in Barcelona, Spain.

Eva is reachable on Twitter, Instagram and over email at embengue9@gmail.com
